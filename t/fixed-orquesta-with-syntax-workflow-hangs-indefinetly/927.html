Indentation and starting whitespace is syntactical in YAML, so make sure your indentation is correct:

guzzijones: 
tasks: add_domains_to_ticket:
  with:
    items: "domain in {{ctx().mitigate_domains }}"
  action: "core.echo message={{item(domain)}}"
