After looking closer at your log:

mongodb-org is already the newest version (4.0.10).

Are you installing StackStorm in a clean VM? Was MongoDB already installed/configured before?

Scripted installer is there for demo/quick deployments and assumes clean/vanilla OS state. Make sure that no other apt sources or packages are added that can clash with the packages needed by StackStorm.

If it’s not a clean VM, I’d suggest to try again on a new one.
